No Pet Turned Away

Here at Paw Spa, our groomers have a combined 30 years of grooming experience. We have been in operations since 2013, and offer all-inclusive grooming services for both dogs and cats. Our products are 98% organic, and we hand-dry every pet. This facility is 100% cage-free! We are proud to offer canine and feline safe essential oils to purchase to assist our clients in continuing the aromatherapy we provide during grooming sessions while at home. We also offer customized aromatherapy products to be incorporated into your pet's grooming session. We pride ourselves in being focused on holistic wellness, and do NOT require our clients to provide proof of vaccination when bringing their pet into our salon, and we encourage every pet owner to make their own educated decision on vaccines for their pets. Call today to book an appointment or find out more about our wellness products from Sarah, our soon to be certified aromatherapist. Our canine massage therapist Jen, is currently offering appointments for massage services. **we are a teaching salon, and our senior groomers regularly educate and train groomers looking to further their education. The wellness and care of the pets visiting our spa is always our top priority during training, and all assistant groomers are supervised by senior groomers during training**
